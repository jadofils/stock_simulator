About the Project

This project is designed to help users practice and learn stock trading without any financial risk. The simulator uses live stock data to simulate the trading experience and helps users understand market behavior and investment strategies.

Features:

  • Real-time stock data integration
  • Track portfolio and investments
  • Analyze historical price trends
  • Simulate buying and selling of stocks
